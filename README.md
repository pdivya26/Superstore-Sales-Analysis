# Superstore Sales Analysis

A fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Superstore sales performance across regions, states, categories, segments, and years. The dashboard provides deep insights into revenue, profit, quantity sold, and customer segments using dynamic filters and visual analytics.

## Key Features

- Interactive sales, profit, and quantity analysis across multiple years (2014â€“2017).
- Dynamic filtering by region, state, order year, category, and segment.
- Yearly and quarterly performance visuals for both sales and profit.
- Profitability breakdown by category and sub-category.
- State-level sales mapping for geographic insights.
- Segment-wise performance comparison (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
- Top-performing cities displayed with quantity and profit metrics.
- Category & sub-category dashboards for detailed product-level analysis.

## Key Insights

- Steady sales growth observed across the years, with strong performance in later quarters.
- Consumer segment drives the highest sales (~50%+), followed by Corporate and Home Office.
- Technology and Office Supplies are the most profitable categories, while Furniture shows comparatively lower profit margins.
- Top cities such as New York, Los Angeles, and Seattle contribute significantly to overall profit.
- California, New York, and Texas generate the highest sales among all states.
- West and East regions outperform others, especially in high-value categories.
- Binders, Accessories, and Appliances show strong sales and quantity figures in sub-category performance.
- Technology category consistently leads in both profit and growth across multiple years.
- Profit is inconsistent across quarters, with some sub-categories (like Furniture) showing occasional losses.
